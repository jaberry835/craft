# Contoso Corporation Pet Bereavement and Bonding Leave Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-2024-PL-042  
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** August 22, 2025

## Purpose

Contoso Corporation recognizes that pets are beloved family members. This policy provides employees with paid time off for pet-related life events.

## Pet Bereavement Leave

### Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed 90 days of service are eligible.

### Leave Allowance by Pet Type

| Pet Category | Bereavement Days | Documentation Required |
|--------------|------------------|------------------------|
| Dogs | 5 days | Veterinary certificate or pet cremation receipt |
| Cats | 5 days | Veterinary certificate or pet cremation receipt |
| Horses | 7 days | Veterinary certificate |
| Exotic Birds (Parrots, Macaws) | 4 days | Veterinary certificate |
| Small Mammals (Rabbits, Hamsters, Guinea Pigs) | 2 days | Self-attestation form |
| Fish | 1 day | Photo of memorial service |
| Reptiles | 3 days | Veterinary certificate |
| Tarantulas and Scorpions | 3 days | Exotic pet specialist certificate |

### Multiple Pet Households
Employees with multiple pets of the same type may only claim bereavement leave once per 6-month period per pet category.

## New Pet Bonding Leave ("Pawternity Leave")

### Overview
When an employee acquires a new pet, they may take paid "Pawternity Leave" to bond with their new family member.

### Allowance
- **Puppies or Kittens (under 1 year):** 5 days
- **Adult Dogs or Cats:** 3 days
- **Rescue Animals (any type):** 7 days (to recognize the additional adjustment period)
- **Fish Tanks (10+ fish):** 1 day for initial setup

### Requirements
- Must be a legally acquired pet (receipt or adoption papers required)
- Leave must be taken within 30 days of pet acquisition
- One Pawternity Leave per employee per calendar year

## The "Emergency Veterinary Flex" Benefit

Employees may take up to **4 hours of paid leave** per month for emergency veterinary appointments without advance notice. Regular check-ups require 24-hour notice.

## Pet Memorial Wall

Contoso maintains a "Rainbow Bridge Memorial Wall" in Building C, Floor 2. Employees may submit a photo and brief tribute (max 50 words) for any pet covered under this policy.

## Fraud Prevention

Falsely claiming pet leave is grounds for disciplinary action. HR reserves the right to:
- Request veterinary records
- Conduct home visits (with 48-hour notice)
- Review social media for evidence of pet existence
