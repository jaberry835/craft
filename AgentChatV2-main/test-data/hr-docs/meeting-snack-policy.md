# Contoso Corporation Meeting Snack Requirements Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-2024-MS-099  
**Effective Date:** April 1, 2024  
**Last Updated:** September 5, 2025

## Overview

Research shows that meetings with snacks are 47% more productive. Therefore, Contoso mandates snack provision for all meetings meeting the criteria below.

## Mandatory Snack Thresholds

| Meeting Duration | Attendees | Snack Requirement |
|-----------------|-----------|-------------------|
| 30-60 minutes | 3+ people | Optional but encouraged |
| 61-90 minutes | Any number | Minimum 2 snack options |
| 91-120 minutes | Any number | Minimum 3 snack options + beverages |
| 2+ hours | Any number | Full snack spread + hot beverage station |
| All-day | Any number | Catered meals + continuous snack access |

## The "Rule of Three" for Snack Variety

When snacks are required, they must include representation from at least THREE of the following categories:

1. **Salty** (chips, pretzels, crackers)
2. **Sweet** (cookies, candy, pastries)
3. **Healthy** (fruit, vegetables, nuts)
4. **Savory** (cheese, meat, hummus)
5. **Caffeinated** (chocolate-covered espresso beans count)
6. **Regional Specialty** (rotating monthly - see Regional Snack Calendar)

## The Ceremonial First Bite

For meetings with 10+ attendees, the meeting organizer must perform the "Ceremonial First Bite" - taking the first snack to signal that others may begin eating. This tradition dates back to Contoso's founding in 1987.

## Budget Allocation

### Per-Person Snack Budget
- Standard meetings: $4.50 per person
- Executive meetings: $8.00 per person
- Client-facing meetings: $12.00 per person
- All-hands meetings: $6.00 per person

### Expense Coding
All snack expenses should be coded to cost center 7734-SNAX with the meeting title in the description.

## Dietary Accommodations

Meeting organizers MUST:
1. Send dietary preference survey 48 hours before the meeting
2. Provide at least one option for each declared restriction
3. Label all snacks with common allergens
4. Maintain a separate "allergy-safe zone" on the snack table

Required accommodation categories:
- Vegetarian
- Vegan
- Gluten-free
- Nut-free
- Dairy-free
- Kosher
- Halal
- Low-sodium

## The "Emergency Snack Cabinet"

Each floor maintains an Emergency Snack Cabinet for impromptu meetings. Contents are restocked every Monday and include:
- Individually wrapped crackers
- Granola bars
- Dried fruit
- Bottled water
- Emergency chocolate (for crisis meetings only)

**Access:** Emergency Snack Cabinets are accessed via badge scan. Usage is logged and charged to the requesting department at $2.00 per person.

## Violations and Consequences

### Snack-Free Meeting Violations
- First offense: Mandatory attendance at "Snack Planning 101" workshop
- Second offense: Meeting scheduling privileges suspended for 2 weeks
- Third offense: Required to provide snacks for another department's meeting

### "Snack Hoarding" Violations
Taking more than a reasonable portion of meeting snacks for personal consumption later is prohibited. Reasonable portion defined as: what can be eaten during the meeting + one additional item.
