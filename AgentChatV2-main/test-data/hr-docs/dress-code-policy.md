# Contoso Corporation Dress Code and Themed Attire Policy

**Policy Number:** HR-2024-DC-015  
**Effective Date:** February 14, 2024  
**Last Updated:** October 1, 2025

## Philosophy

At Contoso, we believe that creative expression through clothing enhances workplace morale and innovation. Our dress code balances professionalism with fun.

## Standard Business Attire (Monday - Wednesday)

Standard business casual applies on Monday through Wednesday:
- Collared shirts, blouses, or company-branded polo shirts
- Dress pants, khakis, or appropriate-length skirts
- Closed-toe shoes
- No visible offensive imagery or text

## Mandatory Theme Days

### Thursday: "Throwback Thursday" (Mandatory)

All employees MUST dress according to a decade theme, rotating monthly:

| Month | Required Decade Theme |
|-------|----------------------|
| January | 1950s |
| February | 1960s |
| March | 1970s |
| April | 1980s |
| May | 1990s |
| June | 2000s |
| July | 1920s |
| August | Medieval Period |
| September | 1940s |
| October | Victorian Era |
| November | Space Age Future |
| December | Employee's Choice |

**Non-compliance penalty:** Employees not in theme must wear the official "I Forgot Throwback Thursday" sash (available from reception) for the entire workday.

### Friday: "Fancy Hat Friday" (Mandatory)

Every Friday, employees must wear a decorative hat. Requirements:
- Minimum hat brim: 3 inches OR minimum hat height: 6 inches
- Hats must be secured (no falling hazards)
- Baseball caps are NOT acceptable unless bedazzled with at least 50 rhinestones
- Novelty hats encouraged but must not obstruct colleagues' views

**Hat Excellence Awards:** Monthly prizes for Best Hat, Most Creative Hat, and Best Team-Coordinated Hats.

## Optional Theme Days

### "Matching Socks Rebellion Day" (First Monday of each month)
Employees are encouraged to wear deliberately mismatched socks. Prizes for most creative combinations.

### "Bring Your Costume to Work Day" (October 31)
Full costumes permitted but must not impair job function or safety.

## Prohibited Attire

The following are never permitted:
- Clothing with profanity or hate symbols
- Beachwear (unless approved for Summer Pool Party event)
- Pajamas (except during approved "Cozy Friday" events)
- Rollerblades or roller skates (except in the parking garage)
- Full mascot costumes that obscure identity during security checks

## The "Penguin Protocol"

During client visits, employees may be asked to wear formal business attire. When this occurs, the code word "Penguin Protocol" will be announced via email at least 24 hours in advance. During Penguin Protocol:
- Full business formal is required
- Theme days are suspended
- Normal operations resume the following day

## Enforcement

Dress code violations are handled with escalating consequences:
1. First offense: Friendly reminder from manager
2. Second offense: Required attendance at "Dress for Success" lunch seminar
3. Third offense: Loss of Fancy Hat Friday participation for one month
4. Fourth offense: HR consultation and documented warning

## Religious and Medical Accommodations

Employees requiring accommodations for religious or medical reasons should contact HR. All reasonable accommodations will be granted, including:
- Alternative headwear for Fancy Hat Friday
- Modified theme participation
- Sensory-friendly fabric allowances
